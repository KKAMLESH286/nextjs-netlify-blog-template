## Fragility/ Robustness/ BlackSwan Event

<!--StartFragment-->

This term is coined by Nassim Taleb, gives the interference between fragility and robustness. Fragility and robustness have more downside than upside when black swan event happens(disorder) whereas Antifragility has more upside than downside as a aftermath of that particular black swan event. So now the question is how do we make us anti fragile? The answer is by putting the skin in the game means we need to participate consistently.
